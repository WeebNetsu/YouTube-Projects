Site Rules

Short Version

  • Nothing illegal
  • Seed

Long Version

It goes without saying, illegal stuff is obviously off-limits. Child pornography, gore, viruses, or anything of the sort is NOT allowed. In terms of torrents, the only torrents you're allowed to add are those containing files that you own (have rights to). If you torrent something, seed it.

This website is just an experiment, if things gets out of hand then I will remove the website forever.

FAQs

Why am I getting an "Internal Server Error"?

The server is very slow and I lack the funds to upgrade it. If you get the error, try reloading the page.

A torrent has no seeds!

Click the report button and the mods will remove it.

How do I stay a good torrent owner?

Simple, seed it as much as possible and if you're the only seed and you want to remove it or lost the file, delete it from the site.

I added a new torrent, but don't see it anywhere.

The server refreshes every hour, it should be visible to everyone then.

Someone asked me for my account details.

No one here will ever ask you for you account details, don't give it to them.

My account is missing.

Your account will automatically be deleted after 6 months of being inactive.

My torrent was removed and I didn't delete it!

Torrents are automatically deleted after 6 months.
